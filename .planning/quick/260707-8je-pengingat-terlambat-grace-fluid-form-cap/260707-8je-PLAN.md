<objective>
Three PO-reported fixes to KidneyBuddy, one task each:

1. Add a 2-minute grace period before the "Terlambat" (overdue) status appears on any reminder. During the grace window show a jenis-appropriate "Segera ..." call-to-action instead. Apply consistently everywhere overdue is displayed.
2. Stop CAPD-only fluid-form fields (Konsentrasi CAPD, Kondisi Cairan Keluar) from leaking into non-CAPD sources — gate them on the selected `sumber`, not on the user's therapy method, and align validation + labels.
3. Correct the daily fluid-balance normal range to be clinically evidence-based (-1000 ml to +500 ml per RESEARCH.md), fire the "cairan tertahan" alert only above +500 ml, update the beranda caption, and darken the "Cairan tertahan" label for readability.

Purpose: Reliability of reminder status and clinical correctness of the fluid-balance signal are core-value surfaces; a false "Terlambat" the instant a reminder is due, and a clinically wrong "cairan tertahan" flag on normal physiological balance, both erode trust.
Output: One shared frontend helper + edits to 9 existing files. Frontend-only behavior changes plus one validation alignment on the backend.
</objective>
