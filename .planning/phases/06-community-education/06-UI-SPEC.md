## Typography

| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Body | 13px | 400 (regular, DM Sans) | 1.5 |
| Label | 12px | 700 (bold, DM Sans) | 1.3 |
| Heading | 14px | 700 (bold, Plus Jakarta Sans) | 1.3 |
| Display | 18px | 700 (bold, Plus Jakarta Sans) | 1.2 |

Exactly 2 weights declared for this phase: **400 regular** (body copy â€” post/reply content, article body, meta timestamps) and **700 bold** (everything else â€” headings, labels, badges, buttons). This matches `DeleteReminderConfirm.tsx`'s established `font-heading font-bold` (title) / `font-sans` (body) split and `AlertHistoryList.tsx`'s `font-heading font-bold` empty-state heading pattern. Do not introduce `font-medium` (500) or `font-semibold` (600) in new Phase 6 components even though earlier phases used them inconsistently â€” this phase standardizes on the 2-weight rule.

Usage mapping:
- **Display (18px/700)**: Post detail page title, education article detail title.
- **Heading (14px/700)**: Post title in feed card, article card title, section headers ("Diskusi Terbaru", "Panduan & Artikel").
- **Label (12px/700)**: Category badges (Pertanyaan/Berbagi Pengalaman/Informasi), therapy-tag badges (CAPD/HD/Transplantasi/Umum), reply count, "membantu" count, timestamps needing emphasis, sub-nav pill text.
- **Body (13px/400)**: Post body preview in feed, full post content, reply content, article body paragraphs, empty-state body copy.
