<objective>
Build the restart-safe reminder dispatch engine: a node-cron job that every minute queries Postgres for due reminders and fans them out to all of a user's devices via the 02-02 notification service, plus a follow-up job that sends a single reminder for doses still unconfirmed after 30 minutes — with boot-time catch-up so a container restart never silently drops reminders.

Purpose: REMIND-02 (push at scheduled time), REMIND-04 (30-min follow-up), and the delivery half of REMIND-08 (caregiver multi-device). The non-negotiable rule: state lives in Postgres queried each tick, never in-memory timers.
Output: scheduler.ts, reminderDispatch.job.ts, reminderFollowUp.job.ts, server.ts wiring, and a dispatch test proving due-selection + fan-out + duplicate-guard.
</objective>

## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** patient (and my caregiver on another device), **I want to** receive a real push notification at each reminder's scheduled time — and a follow-up if I do not confirm — even after the server restarts, **so that** I never miss a dose or therapy session because of an infrastructure hiccup.

- New jobs: `dispatchDueReminders`, `sendFollowUpReminders`, `startScheduler`
- Modified: `server.ts` calls `startScheduler()` after `app.listen()`
- Backend dependency: `node-cron`
