<objective>
Build the profile-settings slice that lets a user change their active therapy method anytime — gated by an explicit confirmation and recorded in therapy history — and replay the onboarding tutorial from Settings.

Purpose: Patients' modality can change over time (e.g., CAPD -> transplant); the app's conditional UI/features must follow, and the change must be auditable (ONBOARD-05). Replaying the tutorial (ONBOARD-06) supports the 50+ persona who may want a refresher. This completes Phase 1's onboarding/identity foundation.
Output: profile page with a confirmation-gated therapy switch that writes history, and a replay-tutorial entry point.
</objective>
